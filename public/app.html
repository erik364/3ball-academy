Privacy Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect
We collect information you provide when creating an account, including your name, email address, phone number, and information about your child(ren). We also collect attendance plans, RSVP responses, and payment status within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information
Your information is used solely to operate the 3Ball Academy program — including scheduling practices and tournaments, tracking attendance plans, communicating with families, and processing payments. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Children's Privacy
We collect limited personal data about players (name, graduation year, team) to manage the program. We do not knowingly collect sensitive data from children under 13 beyond what is necessary to operate the program. Parents or guardians must create the account on behalf of their child.

4. Data Storage & Security
Your data is stored securely. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention
We retain your account information for as long as you remain an active participant in the program, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Your Rights
You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting 3Ball Academy directly.

7. Contact
For privacy-related questions, contact us at info@3ballacademy.com.
